Padiyapelella Mini Hydropower Project operates with a capacity of 3.5MW utilizing the water resource from Beliul Oya at Padiyapelella in Nuwara Eliya District. PHL has entered into Standardised Power Purchase Agreement (SPPA) with the Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) to supply all of its energy generated to the national grid. PHL plant was commissioned on March 01, 2017. Padiyapelella MHP generates 13.26Gwh of average annual energy.
